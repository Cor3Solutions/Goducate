Why We Do It

Millions of needy people in Asia have no access to the basic necessities of life and a useful education. They are at risk of diseases and vices, and lacking hope and purpose in their lives. A timely help can give them a chance of progress and an opportunity to break away from the poverty cycle.

With your support, we will be able to make a difference in their lives.

Arthur Defensor Jr.
Governor, Iloilo Province

Hear It From Our Partners

Through the years, Camp Goducate Phils., Inc. has touched many lives through its various community development programs not only in Iloilo province but in other parts of the Philippines and all over Asia. Despite the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, Camp Goducate Phils., Inc. has managed to train world-class Community Development Workers (CDWs) and continued to provide trainings and educational programs for various sectors. Moreover, I commend Camp Goducate for embarking on a nationwide Feeding and Educational program to help our less fortunate citizens. Indeed, our society needs organizations like Camp Goducate that bring help and hope to the needy and empower ordinary people to create positive change. It is my wish that Camp Goducate Phils., Inc. will continue to touch more lives through your various community development programs.
