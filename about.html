WHO WE ARE

GODUCATE

Goducate is a non-profit organization formed to reach children in poor communities in Asia and close the poverty gap by working closely with (not just for) them.

We believe that every child deserves a chance to learn. With access to quality education and care for their basic needs, children and their families are given a path toward a better and brighter future.

About the Founder

A medical doctor by training, Dr. Paul Choo founded Goducate in 2007 after recognizing the immense needs in Asia’s underserved communities.

He believed — and still believes — that education is the key to changing a child’s life. But beyond that, he saw the value of helping communities in a way that was not just temporary, but sustainable and life-changing.

This belief continues to guide Goducate today.

Our Mission

To see vulnerable children in Asia empowered through education to fulfill their potential and purpose in life — and extend a helping hand to others who may be in a similar situation in the future.

Our Vision

To GO to the neediest and EDUCATE.
We create initiatives and build meaningful partnerships that improve the health and well-being of poverty-stricken children across Asia.

OUR BOARD

We are governed by an Advisory Board who share a deep commitment to Goducate’s goals and mission.

Dr. Paul Choo

Founder & Chair of Goducate
Founding Pastor & Missions Chair of Gospel Light Christian Church (Singapore)

Dr. Paul Choo is a medical doctor by training. He co-founded Shenton Medical Group in 1973, which became one of Singapore’s largest medical networks. In 1995, he sold the group to a public-listed healthcare company and entered full-time Christian service.

He is the founding pastor of Gospel Light Christian Church, where he served for 20 years while actively helping poor communities in Asia. In 2007, he stepped down from pastoring to focus entirely on Goducate’s work.

He currently chairs the Missions Board at GLCC and continues to pour his life into helping poor Asians help themselves. In 2011, he was named one of Channel News Asia’s “Asians of the Year” for his humanitarian work.

Leong Lick Tien

Vice-Chair of Goducate
Founding Elder of Gospel Light Christian Church (Singapore)

Leong Lick Tien has had a distinguished career in the Singapore public service, holding senior leadership roles across various agencies. He retired in 2007 as Deputy CEO of the Central Provident Fund Board.

He is a founding elder at Gospel Light Christian Church and serves on its Missions Board. He is also actively involved in several nonprofit organizations committed to empowering communities across Asia through self-sustainable development.

Victor Yeo

Vice-Chair of Goducate
Founding Elder of Gospel Light Christian Church (Singapore)

Victor Yeo is a pioneer in private equity and venture capital in Asia. He served as Vice President of the Private Investment Company for Asia (PICA) and later as Executive Director managing multi-million dollar funds across Southeast Asia.

After retiring from fund management, Victor devoted himself to full-time voluntary work in the church and with Goducate. He currently serves as a pastor at Gospel Light Christian Church and plays an active leadership role in Goducate’s mission.
