GO CAMP FARM

Empowering Communities Through Sustainable Farming & Education

Goducate Farm & Camp in Iloilo focuses on practical training and capacity-building to help marginalized communities become self-sufficient and improve their quality of life.

Our Goals

  • To alleviate poverty by equipping communities with practical skills.
  • To promote self-sufficiency and sustainability.
  • To improve the quality of life for marginalized groups.

Agricultural Training

Learn sustainable farming methods like organic farming, crop rotation, and water conservation with hands-on experience.

Animal Husbandry

Training in raising chickens, goats, and cows, with emphasis on animal health, productivity, and farm income.

Community Development

Programs that empower locals to increase food security, income, and cooperation through shared knowledge.

Educational Programs

Basic education and vocational training for both children and adults, focusing on literacy and life skills.

Camp Facilities

A venue for workshops, seminars, and community events, with accommodations for visitors and volunteers.
